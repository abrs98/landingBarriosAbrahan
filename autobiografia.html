About me....

I am originally from the state of Sinaloa, specifically from the municipality of Angostura,
place where agriculture, fishing and livestock are the primary activities
that generate the main source of economic income for families. I was born on 3 December 1998 in Mazatlán.


Son of a farmer and a housewife, I grew up in a ranch called El llano, a very hospitable and quiet place surrounded by lands
agricultural located 10 kilometers from the coast. I attended elementary school there and
later I studied secondary school in the municipal seat, as well as high school
(UAS high school). High school was one of the best stages, because of friends and things
what I learned. Although I was not very good academically, I felt a certain degree of
satisfaction for contributing something to my family and social environment. I was in the Red Cross,
as part of my service and I saw from another perspective the risk situations at the
that we are all exposed, and how we should act. I really like sports, sports soccer and volleyball above all. I spent a year in Arizona with the purpose of learning
English, it was a very productive and profitable year, because for the first time, I had the experience of interacting with people from different parts of the world
and learn a little about their lifestyles and customs. I study engineering career
software, because I have always liked computers. I took the summer course
offered by ITSON, and I hope to learn how to make my own mobile applications in this course
to an acceptable level. I will do my best during this class and throughout the race
to graduate as a good professional, because I want to contribute to society. for I would like to work in the area of ​​creation of management computer systems,
